# Wayanad Landslide Analysis 2024

## Overview
This repository presents a geospatial and disaster management analysis of the 2024 Wayanad landslide disaster in Kerala, India. The project examines the causes, impacts, rescue operations, and environmental vulnerability associated with the landslide event.

---

## Study Area
- Wayanad District
- Kerala, India
- Western Ghats Region

---

## Objectives
- Study the causes of the Wayanad landslide
- Analyze environmental and climatic vulnerability
- Understand rainfall-triggered slope failures
- Examine disaster response and rescue operations
- Explore disaster management strategies

---

## Key Topics Covered
- Landslide vulnerability
- Extreme rainfall events
- Climate change impacts
- Western Ghats ecology
- Disaster response management
- Relief camp management
- Search and rescue operations
- Waste and logistics management

## Key Findings
- Intense rainfall triggered massive slope failure
- Land-use change increased vulnerability
- Settlements in high-risk zones were heavily affected
- Improved preparedness and mitigation are necessary
